So... What does SoundOff do?

We bring original music to you by acting as the "tinder of music". For Artists, their music will be front of more listeners. For listeners, music will come to you with the swipe of a finger.

Who would like SoundOff?

The answer is Everybody! Whether you're an upcoming artist, a local venue owner, or simply an avid music lover, SoundOff is right for you.

Music Lovers

Tired of hearing the same songs over and over again? Having trouble finding new music? With as many filters as you like, be it genre, location, or mood, you will be presented with a constant supply of new music awaiting your judgment. Go discover those hidden gems!

Artists

Tired of 0 likes? 0 views? Maybe 1 or 2 from you mom or friend? Current media players recycle and advertise the music that is already popular, often leaving the vast majority of music on any given platform unseen. Though we can not guarantee your music to be a hit, SoundOff guarantees that someone will at least listen to what you have to offer.

Venue Owner

Struggling to find a last minute artist for your party? Looking for a jazz trio to play at your cafe? Failing to find an adequate singer for your soul-funk college band? With the filters provided (location, genre, etc.), a User can discover talented artist in their area, and effortlessly connect to these artists through SoundOff.

How does SoundOff work?

Your SoundOff experience will first begin by creating a User account for yourself. From there, with the selection of as many filters as you like, or none!, you will be presented with a randomly chosen song from a collection of new and upcoming artists. Listening to the music as long as you like, you will either swipe left or right depending on whether you like it or not. Once you swipe, another song will be presented, once again awaiting your judgement. Want to see what your friends and others think of the new music? Updated charts and lists will reflect the most liked music for any given genre!
